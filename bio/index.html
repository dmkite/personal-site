My name is Dylan. I'm a history buff, comic book nerd, artist, developer, and community worker. I'm on a mission to use my creative flair and technical skills to make folk's lives better, to leave the world better than I found it, and inspire people to learn something new.

2013

Freedom School Partners

To combat summer loss of learning and diminish economically based educational disparity I joined a team of diverse and passionate educators - an experience that established my interest in social justice in education, kick starting what would become a rich history of working with community-facing non-profits.

2015

Graduation

In May of 2015 I graduated from the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill - taking with me an unorthodox and dynamic skillset having studied history, education, and information systems.

AmeriCorps and Beyond

Having moved to Ann Arbor, MI, and determined to serve my community I embarked on a service year inspiring children to learn, securing food for hungry families, and using my fluency with technology to boost the capacity of Community Action Network.

2016

Web Development Adventures

Inspired to overhaul the Washtenaw Area Council for Children's website, I taught myself the HTML and CSS skills necessary to actualize my goal. Along the way I discovered a passion for development and pursued formal education to broaden my skills.

2018

Seattle

With a few websites under my belt and a growing library of digital choose your own adventure games, I'm eager to do more and excited to see what unfolds next!
