Alt: Aloof, water color and pen & ink, 2017

Escaping Monotony

Perhaps better described as analog graphic design than art, my body of work is a dichotomous interplay between radiant, flamboyant technicolor and flat monochrome. In each work splashes of color hint at a beautiful reality beyond our own, igniting our primordial need to escape monotony. Want to buy a print? Comission a piece? Drop me a line.
